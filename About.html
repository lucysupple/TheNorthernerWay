How the story began

For years I've wanted to see the world, ever since seeing my dad's photos from his time in Australia. When my sister went to Auz, the cravings to leave got bigger, but I satified those needs by going on regular holidays to Europe. But with the ending on my degree in 2020, I decided that I couldn't put it off any longer, and started planning and saving for my adventure of a lifetime.
